GR-Systems and Instruments

Business Development Analysis

Conducting comprehensive analysis for identifying barriers and creating optimal conditions for business development.

Key Components:

  • Systematic barrier identification and assessment
  • Market analysis and opportunity mapping
  • Regulatory impact studies
  • Business ecosystem optimization strategies
03

Regulatory Improvement

Developing recommendations and legislative initiatives to improve regulatory and legal frameworks.

Key Components:

  • Policy analysis and reform recommendations
  • Legislative impact assessment
  • Regulatory sandbox development
  • Stakeholder engagement mechanisms

International Cooperation

Building partnerships and international connections for experience exchange and implementing best international practices.

Key Components:

  • Global research network development
  • Cross-border knowledge transfer programs
  • International standards adoption
  • Multilateral project participation
